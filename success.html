1. Wealth Increased

Mr.Harjibhai Bhikhabhai

I am a small farmer.I live in village Nana Kandhasar.Under the Gujarat Government Watershed Project we formed S.H.G of 12 farmers.We regularly deposited our savings in bank and then we got revolving fund from Shri Aayurved Gramodyog Mandal. We all attended Employment Generation one day seminar arranged by this NGO and it inspired me to start a small Flourmill .Currently I am getting 80 to 100 rupees per day from this Flourmill.This is happened only because of this NGO.

2. Upbringing Lives

Mr. Samantbhai Bavalya

I am of 38 years and live in Bamanbohre.I am the active member of Self Help Group which is formed by Shri Aayurved Gramodyog Mandal.During the S.H.G training I got inspiration to start soft drink making then I got loan from my Self Help Group,I also got Revolving Fund from Shri Aayurved Gramdyog Mandal and I started soft drink making and now every month I am getting around 7000 rupees from this business.This is only due to active support of above NGO.

3. Upbringing Lives

Mr. Raju Hamir

I live in village Nana Kandhasar. My family was considered as "Antyoday" ( One of the five families whose income is the lowest in the village) But, under the water shed development project, Shri Aayurved Gramodyog Mandal constructed Sim Talav with west weir near my barren land in Survey no 109. Now, my land is no longer barren and is helping me earn a handsome income. I am extremely thankful to the people from Shri Aayurved Gramodyog Mandal.
