Health Camps And Sanitization

Every year our Organisation arranges two medical camp for Rural People.During this camp we call Pediatrician,Dentist,Gynaecologist,Phyicisian and Skin specialist doctors.We also provide free medicines and food packets to beneficiaries for above camp.We have sponsorships from donors. Every year we arrange two camps in rural area for Animal Care.During this camp we call veterinary doctors,we provide required medicines for free to all the animals.For above camp we have prominent donors.
