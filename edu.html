Education & Training

• Three days training for Vegetable garden projects.We already provide vegetables kit to beneficiaries.

• One week training for Dairy Development Activity for male and female.

• With the help of Food Department we arrange 15 days training for making of different types of pickels,sauce,jam and food preservation techniques.

• One week training for female for Dress Designing.

• Our Organisation does series of training programes for rural people.It is like 6 weeks Sivan training for female tailorings and scheduling clothes with co-operation from District rural development agency

• Our organisation arranges 3 months training for Handi Crafts and Embroidery work for rural female.

• Every month one day our organisation volunteers spend one day in rural area’s primary school and Aanganvadi school and try to develop good habits in them.

• Every three months we arrange seminar for farmers , during this we call an Agricultural expert.They give guidance for latest Agriculture techniques. Fertiliser company gives sponsorship for above seminar.

• With the co-operation of Navgujarat Health Foundation our organisation arranged one month basic computer training for rural youth,120 youth (95 Boys and 25 Girls)attended 90 hours class,at Chotila and our organisation decided to arrange every year one month free class for rural youth and make them computer literate and there by increase employment opportunity.

• Oue NGO got authorisation for the project named “SCOPE”(Society for creation of opportunity through proficiency in english) run by Gujarat Government education department and we run certified English language training center for urban youth at surendranagar.

• Under this project we trained 3200 students in last five years and there by we tried to increase their communication skills and tried to make them employed.

• Gujarat Government Rojgar And Talim Department also authenticated our English Language Training Center and send 1950 unemployed youth for English language training and also gave stipend to all those trained youth.

• Currently 280 girls are getting training from our training center and they are sponsored by R & T Department
