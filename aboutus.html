About Us


  • Shri Aayurved Gramodhyog Mandal is a Non-Government, Non-Profit making organisation registered under “Trust and Societies Act” in 1993. Right from our registration, our organisation is working for rural community, implementing enhancement program for livelihood and management of human resources.

    Our NGO is also taking care of men, animals, water land and plans by educating village people, implementing skill development projects and making them aware of the modern technologies.

    We even organise various “Nidaan and Saarvaar” Health Camps, Cattle Breeding Education Camps, etc. with the co-operation of Government Department and other experts. With proper watershed management programs like building check-dams, Sim talav, Bori-bandh, Khet-talavdi, we help the farmers generate income and make their lands fertile.

    We also participate in large scale plantation with the help of forest department and various self help groups. Shri Aayurved Gramodhyog Mandal has an experience of 19 years in various rural development activities.
